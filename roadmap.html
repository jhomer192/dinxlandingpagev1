Roadmap

Dinx Roadmap

September 1st 2022:

On September 1st, we will drop our NFT membership. There will be three levels that can be minted from the website. The three levels are Founder, VIP, and Contributor. These will be designated by the color of their background. Each NFT will also contain unique are of a Pickleball paddle.


Different benefits of each membership

Founder:

  • Access to the Founder and VIP lounges in the metaverse club
  • Access to all other areas available to holders in the virtual club
  • The ability to visit any physical Dinx Club with three guests free of charge
  • 3 voting points in the community


    • VIP:

  • Access to the VIP lounges in the metaverse club
  • Access to all other areas available to holders in the metaverse club
  • The ability to visit a Dinx Club specified by the holder with one free guests (can't be switched after designation)
  • 2 voting points in the community


    • Contributor:

  • Access to areas exclusive to holders in the metaverse club
  • The ability to visit a Dinx Club specified by the holder (can't be switched after designation)
  • 1 voting points in the community


    • October 1st, 2022:

    On October 1st, the Dinx community will be able to vote on logistics for the virtual club. Some of the items being voted upon will be the location of clubs, amenities included for members, community development, club rules, and more. Think of the community votes as shaping the members experience at the club while the team behind Dinx is managing the backend logistics to keep the club afloat.


    November/December 2022:

    Dinx drops merchandise including clothing, shoes, paddles, and more. NFT holders will receive discounts based on the tier of their membership. NFT holders can also purchase custom paddles based on their unique paddle NFT art. The profits from the merchandise will be reinvested back into building a physical club.


    Early 2023:

    Dinx pickleball club opens in the metaverse. Location and platform voted upon by community members. More details will be revealed at a future date.


    Late 2023/Early 2024:

    Dinx prepares to expand merchandise lines, allowing for merch to be bought in physical stores and officially opens first of many physical clubs around the country.
