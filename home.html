About Us

Dinx is a project about bringing Web3 and Pickleball together. Dinx plans on doing this by releasing 7,777 NFTs on the Ethereum Network. Each NFT will be a unique Pickleball paddle, which is then a physical copy of the holders' unique paddle, which can be custom ordered through Dinx. Dinx will also sell high-end Pickleball items (athletic clothes, generic paddles, balls, bags, etc.) These NFTs also give the holders access to a Pickleball Club in the metaverse and country clubs centered around Pickleball in the physical world.

The Pickleball clubs, in the metaverse and the physical world, will be primarily controlled by the holders of the NFT. Holders of the NFT will vote on everything related to the clubs except for back-end logistics (such as Utilities, Maintenance Staff, Etc.) This allows the holders to shape the Pickleball clubs of their dreams. Dinx will use the money made from the Dinx eCommerce Pickleball store and the sale of the NFTs to raise money to create the club in the metaverse and the club in the physical world. We plan to use profits from physical clubs to set up more Pickleball centered country clubs worldwide.
